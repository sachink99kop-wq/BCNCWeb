Who We Are

Engineering Trust, One Component at a Time

Belgaum CNC Pvt Ltd is a precision machining company based in Belagavi, Karnataka. We specialise in subcontract CNC, VMC and HMC machining for OEMs, fabricators and manufacturers who need reliable capacity and consistent quality. From single prototypes to repeat production batches, we machine to your drawings and your tolerances.

Beyond machining alone, we handle casting procurement and complete processing — sourcing quality castings, then carrying out all required machining operations in-house. This single-point responsibility removes coordination headaches and keeps your supply chain lean.

With a strong record in both domestic and export sales, we deliver finished, inspection-ready components to customers across India and overseas — on specification and on time.
