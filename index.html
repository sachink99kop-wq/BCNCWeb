Who We Are

Engineering Trust, One Component at a Time

Belgaum CNC Applications Pvt Ltd was established in 1989 as a CNC machine shop for job work, based in Udyambag, Belagavi, Karnataka. We machine components on CNC machines for the general engineering industry — compressors, separators, engines, earth-moving equipment, pumps and valves, and more.

Our robust machining infrastructure includes 9 Horizontal Machining Centers, 2 Vertical Machining Centers, 1 CNC Vertical Turn-Mill Center and 3 CNC Turning Centers, backed by strong in-house inspection — CMM, Trimos & Digi-Mahr 2D height gauges, surface finish tester and a full range of precision gauges. As of today we machine about 250 different types of cast components in SG Iron and Cast Iron, with batch sizes from 25 to 500 pieces per month.

An ISO 9001:2015 certified company with a team of around 50 people, we began exports in 2017 to Atlas Copco Airpower, Belgium, and from November 2025 to PRIMAX Pumps, Dubai. Over 50 components reach global customers such as Atlas Copco, Alfa Laval, Caterpillar, JCB and Wärtsilä through our major partners AKP Foundries and AKP Ferrocast.
